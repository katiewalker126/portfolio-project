Hi, I am Katie Walker

I am an aspiring front-end developer based in Sheffield, UK

My interest in coding started to develop within my current job role. I am an Administration Manager for a software development company based in Sheffield. I work in the operations side of the business in areas such as finance, recruitment, human resources, health & safety and general office administration.

Since working in this role from January 2019, I have increasingly become interested in the software development side of the business and after some research I discovered the wonderful SheCodes, offering online coding workshops for women. After enrolling onto the introductory class, I quickly signed up to the SheCodes Basics workshop followed by SheCodes Plus and SheCodes Pro (a bundle package including SheCodes Responsive and SheCodes React).

The SheCodes workshops have been invaluable. I am so glad I took that first step signing up to the initial class and am excited to continue with my coding journey.
